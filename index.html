About Aignite

Hackathon AIgnite is a high-energy, AI-focused hackathon where innovation takes center stage. Designed for passionate developers, tech enthusiasts, and problem-solvers, AIgnite challenges participants to push the limits of artificial intelligence and technology. The event is jointly organised by Department of Computer Science and Engineering, in association with Training and Skill Development Cell, and Start-up, Innovation and Entrepreneurship Cell. Our goal is to foster creativity, collaboration, and groundbreaking solutions by bringing together talented minds from diverse backgrounds. With intense coding sessions, expert mentorship, and real-world problem statements, AIgnite provides an exciting platform to develop AI-driven solutions that make a difference. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, AIgnite is your chance to compete, learn, and showcase your AI expertise. Join us and ignite the future of technology!
